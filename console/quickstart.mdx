### Sign Up

Navigate to the [Thunder Compute console](https://console.thundercompute.com/signup) and create an account with your email.

### Add a Payment Method

In the console, [add a payment method](https://console.thundercompute.com/settings/billing) to your account.

### Create an Instance

1. Click **Create Instance** on the dashboard
2. Select your mode, GPU, CPU, disk size, and template
3. Click **Launch**

### Connect to Your Instance

The console displays connection information for your instances. Copy the SSH command and run it in your local terminal:

```bash
ssh user@<instance-ip>
```

For easier access with automatic key management, use the [CLI](/cli/quickstart) or [VSCode extension](/vscode/quickstart).

That's it\! You're now ready to use Thunder Compute.
